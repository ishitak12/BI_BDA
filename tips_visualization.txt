Based on your tips dataset (7 columns: total_bill, tip, sex, smoker, day, time, size, tip_pct), here are all the meaningful visualizations you can perform:

📊 Comparison Charts

Bar/Column Chart — Avg tip by day, sex, time (Lunch vs Dinner), or smoker
Grouped Bar Chart — Avg tip by day grouped by sex or smoker side by side
Stacked Bar Chart — Total tips stacked by sex across each day


🔵 Distribution Charts

Histogram — Distribution of tip or total_bill amounts (how frequently each range occurs)
Box Plot — Spread of tip across day or sex — shows median, outliers, and quartiles
Dot Plot — Each individual tip plotted by day to see clustering


🔗 Relationship Charts

Scatter Plot — total_bill vs tip — reveals correlation (bigger bill = bigger tip?)
Scatter with color — Same scatter but color-coded by smoker or sex to spot group patterns
Bubble Chart — total_bill (X), tip (Y), size (bubble size) — 3 variables at once


🥧 Part-to-Whole Charts

Pie / Donut Chart — Share of visits by day, sex, or smoker status
Treemap — Total tips broken down by day and time simultaneously


📈 Trend & Pattern Charts

Line Chart — Avg tip across days (Thur → Fri → Sat → Sun) to show weekend trend
Area Chart — Same as line but filled, good for showing total tip volume over days


🔥 Advanced / Analytical Charts

Heatmap (Conditional Formatting) — day vs time matrix colored by avg tip % — instantly spots the best tipping slot
Trendline on Scatter — Add a linear trendline with R² to quantify how strongly bill size predicts tip
PivotChart with Slicers — Dynamic chart that filters by smoker, sex, or time interactively
Combo Chart — Avg tip (bars) + count of visits (line) on dual axis per day
